Hey, I'm Venkatesh!

I am a computer vision researcher, software developer and a musician.

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. with the Image Processing and Computer Vision Group (IPCV) and the Music Technology Group (MTG) at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona, under the supervision of Prof. Gloria Haro and Prof. Emilia Gómez. My research primarily focuses on using the information from the audio-visual modalities to train and develop AI systems to perform intelligent tasks that involve deep understanding of the audio and the video content like music source separation.

Interests
