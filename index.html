I received my Ph.D. at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF) , Barcelona, under the supervision of Prof. Gloria Haro. I bring extensive experience and expertise in transforming Proof of Concepts (PoCs) into Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) in the fields of Generative AI, Computer Vision, and Audio, leveraging advanced Deep Learning techniques. My expertise includes designing, training, fine-tuning, and repurposing deep learning models.


With over eight years of Python programming experience, seven years working with PyTorch, and two years with TensorFlow and Keras, I have developed and deployed diverse deep learning architectures, ranging from CNNs to Transformers and Diffusion models. Additionally, I am proficient in creating interactive WebUIs using Streamlit and Gradio to showcase audio-visual demos for clients, managing and tracking experiments with MLFlow, and containerizing applications with Docker.

I am currently seeking opportunities as a Senior AI Engineer, where I can contribute my skills to innovative and impactful projects.
