I started training in vocal Indian classical music at the age of 8. Besides being trained in vocal music, I also play the bansuri – a transverse flute made of bamboo from the Indian sub-continent, harmonium and the piano. I am currently training bansuri under the guidance of the world reknowned musician Pt. Pravin Godkhindi. I have been fortunate to collaborate and work with musicians around the world in various musical projects.
